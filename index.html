About Us

G.C Study & Work Abroad Consulting Limited was established with a clear mission: to make educational opportunities accessible. Five years ago, we dedicated ourselves to this goal, and it continues to be our primary focus. Our advisory services are free, and our counselors are trained to match prospective students with the best options for their future.

see more

We value personal relationships and won't offer advice until we fully understand your needs. Once we have developed your plan, we'll assist with applications, accommodation choices, and visa processes. As a comprehensive resource for students seeking opportunities abroad, our expertise has positioned us as the leading game changer in education consultancy in East Africa.

Our Vission

G.C. Study & Work Abroad Consulting Limited aspires to become the foremost student recruitment agency in East Africa. We are dedicated to offering precise and relevant information, maintaining trustworthy and transparent interactions, and effectively managing and coordinating students' transitions to their destination countries. Furthermore, we will implement strategic marketing initiatives to enhance our partners' visibility and drive the growth of our company.

Our Mission

Our mission is to engage and recruit international students globally, with a particular emphasis on East Africa, by leveraging the Internet and social media networks. We aim to connect students with higher education opportunities abroad, building our reputation as the most reliable student recruitment agency in Africa.

Why Choose Our Products?

Choosing us means partnering with a team dedicated to your success. We provide personalized guidance to help you identify and gain admission to the university that best matches your academic and career goals. Our extensive global network connects you with top institutions, offering a wide range of educational opportunities.
